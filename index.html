About this event

The Theme for  his year’s Ladies conference is “The 360° Lady”, which defines a complete, whole and thriving lady in various pellets of life such as spirituality, relationships, health, career, finances and many more. This program is aimed to strengthen Ladies to walk in God ultimate truth of completeness in him.

We will be teaching our ladies how to develop a balanced life while leading in all areas of life. There would also be different practical sessions of learning various life hacks as a Lady.
